How income reaches you

The income types Ztor pays out · tap to expand

Income from selling merch, bundles, auctions or digital goods in your E-Shop.

Ticket sales, sign-up fees and other event-related income from Events.

Support raised once your co-creation project is confirmed, released to you in stages.

Royalties when your own IP is rented, referenced or reused.

Once a project you backed is released, your share of its OTT royalty income by stake.

Your share of a music project's distributor royalty statements (streaming / downloads); compiled periodically, not real-time.

Streaming, ad or sync revenue synced from external platforms like YouTube and Spotify.

When your review drives a sale, a cut by your personal commission rate, applied to net income.

An advance Ztor pays a tastemaker upfront, offset against later commission.

Licensing fees, rentals, winning bids and other licensing-contract income. Paid as a share of net profit.

Splits, sponsorships or brand-deal income you earn as a collaborator.

Splits from Fanvestor / fan-investment mechanisms.

Reward money from platform campaigns and milestones, e.g. contest prizes and promo bonuses.

Profit share from co-creation parties you host or join (screenings, meetups and other in-person events).

Proposed integration. Tastemaker, OTT / music royalty and co-creation income types are proposed additions (ASSUMPTIONS CCR-001), not yet confirmed product behaviour.
