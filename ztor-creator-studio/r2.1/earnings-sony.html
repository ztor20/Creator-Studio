How it works

Deposit types

Co-creation
Support raised once a co-creation project you started is confirmed, released to you in stages.
OTT royalties
After a project you backed is released, your share of the OTT royalty income by equity stake.
Music royalties
Your share of distributor royalty statements (streaming / downloads) for music projects you started or backed; statements are batched in periodically, not real-time.
Tastemaker commission
Spending driven by your reviews, returned at your personal commission rate; applies to net income only.
Tastemaker advance
An advance the platform pays tastemakers up front, offset against later commission.
Licensing
Income from licensing and derivative use of your original IP on the platform. This type is shared on a net-profit basis — payouts begin only after related costs are deducted and net profit is actually made.
Bonus
Reward money from platform campaigns and milestones — e.g. creation-contest prizes and promotion incentives.
Co-creation party
Profit-share income from co-creation parties (screenings, meet-ups and other in-person events) you host or join.
